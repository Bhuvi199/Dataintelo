While the soccer balls market is set for growth, there are challenges that could hinder its progress. One of the significant challenges is the price sensitivity in emerging markets. High-quality soccer balls, especially those used in professional leagues, can be expensive, limiting their accessibility in price-conscious regions.

Additionally, the supply chain disruptions caused by the ongoing global trade issues and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic have created uncertainty in the market. Manufacturers are struggling to meet the demand for soccer balls, particularly during peak seasons.
