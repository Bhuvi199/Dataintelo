The global seaweed snacks market is experiencing a significant surge, driven by a growing trend towards healthier snacking options. With increasing awareness about the nutritional benefits of seaweed, consumers are turning to these snacks as a healthier alternative to traditional options. The market, valued at USD 1.5 billion in 2023, is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period 2023-2030, reaching a projected value of USD 3.2 billion by 2030.

One of the primary drivers of this market is the rising demand for plant-based and organic snacks. Consumers are becoming more health-conscious, seeking snacks that offer nutritional benefits without compromising on taste. Seaweed snacks, rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, perfectly align with these consumer preferences.
